NPK Information

Nitrogen (N)

Essential for leaf and stem growth. Promotes healthy green foliage and overall plant vigor.

Phosphorus (P)

Important for root development, flowering, and fruit production. Enhances energy transfer.

Potassium (K)

Improves fruit quality, disease resistance, and water regulation. Essential for tomato ripening.

Growth Stage Tips

Vegetative Growth

Higher nitrogen levels support leaf and stem growth. Ensure adequate sunlight and water.

Flowering Stage

Increase phosphorus to support flower formation. Reduce nitrogen to prevent excessive foliage.

Fruiting Stage

Higher potassium levels improve fruit quality and size. Maintain consistent watering.
